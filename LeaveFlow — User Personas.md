# LeaveFlow — User Personas

## Ana Popescu — The Employee
- Age: 28 | Role: Software Developer | Seniority: Mid | 3 years at company
- Submits leave 3–4 times per year, mostly annual and occasional sick days
- Needs: see remaining days at a glance, submit quickly, get confirmation
- Pain points: doesn't know her balance without asking HR, gets no response on status for days

## Mihai Ionescu — The Manager
- Age: 38 | Role: Engineering Team Lead | Manages: 8 developers
- Receives 4–6 leave requests per month
- Needs: see who's out and when, approve without opening email threads, spot team overlap
- Pain points: misses requests buried in email, finds out about conflicts only after approving

## Ioana Dumitrescu — The HR Admin
- Age: 35 | Role: People Operations Specialist | Manages: 120 employees
- Configures policies, handles escalations, produces monthly reports
- Needs: central visibility, easy policy updates, accurate exportable data
- Pain points: manual spreadsheet corrections, chasing managers who don't respond, no audit trail

## Radu Constantin — The Edge Case Employee
- Age: 24 | Role: Junior Developer | 6 months at company
- Has almost exhausted his annual leave balance (6 days remaining out of 20)
- Useful for testing: insufficient balance scenarios, pro-rated allocation, carry-over edge cases
